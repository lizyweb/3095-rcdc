ABOUT US

Rural and Community Development Center (RCDC Trust)

RURAL AND COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT CENTRE (RCDC TRUST) is a service-minded voluntary and non-governmental organization established in 2024 and registered in the sub-registrar office in Kattumannarkoil with Reg. No: 04/2024 under the Indian Trust Act 1882. Our mission is to address ignorance, illiteracy, and exploitation, which are major hindrances for socially deprived and disadvantaged people, and to improve their standard of life through various support programs.

We strive to empower women and children by providing education, vocational training, and access to healthcare. By fostering a sense of community and self-reliance, we aim to create sustainable development and long-term social change. Our holistic approach ensures that all aspects of an individual's well-being are addressed, promoting a more inclusive and equitable society. Join us in our mission to uplift the underprivileged and make a lasting impact on their lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to cater all the women and children, adolescents, senior citizen, physically mentally challenged people, orphan people, minority people, transgender peoples and oppressed rural, urban rural, urban poor people from discrimination and protect their rights towards better development.

Our Mission

Input resource for the better social Economical, cultural and Educational advancement with the sensitization of the people about their right and limitations.

Provide information to the people on Education Development, Social participation and Environment and good Ecological management.

Create opportunity to the people to adopt good health education, hunger and disease, through awareness camp and dissemination of information for their development.

Our Future Plans

Fulfilling the needs of physically handicapped people.

Using Nature Agriculture and also using Nature Medicines.

Starting Guidelines Training centre and train them in higher Education (TET, TRB, TNPSC, IAS, IPS).

Distributing the prizes and awards to the successful students in 10th & 12th in Govt. Examinations.

Conducting Eye Camp and Sugar Medical Camp.
