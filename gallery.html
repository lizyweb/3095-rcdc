Rural and Community Development Center (RCDC Trust)

RCDC Trust Established in 2024 and registered in Kattumannarkoil under the Indian Trust Act 1882 (Reg. No: 04/2024), RCDC Trust is dedicated to uplifting socially deprived and disadvantaged communities. Our mission focuses on combating ignorance, illiteracy, and exploitation. We empower women and children through education, vocational training, and healthcare access, fostering sustainable development and social change. Join us in creating an inclusive, equitable society and making a lasting impact on lives.
